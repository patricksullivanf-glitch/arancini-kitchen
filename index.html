About

Meet the Kitchen

Kenny De Almeida, Founder & Executive Chef, grew up immersed in Italian American culinary tradition and a lifelong passion for food, where great food was at the heart of every gathering. Inspired by his father, who spent over 30 years as the head chef of a well-known Italian restaurant in the Greater Boston area, Kenny developed an appreciation for authentic Italian cuisine from an early age.

With 15 years of experience in the restaurant industry, Kenny seeks to deliver exceptional food and memorable dining experiences. Following in his father's footsteps while bringing his own creativity and vision to the table, he founded The Arancini Kitchen to offer a unique twist on classic Italian street food — combining tradition, quality, and bold flavors in every dish.
